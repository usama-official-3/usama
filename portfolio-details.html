Laundry Care App - Spin Cycle Solutions

This is a mobile application developed for a comprehensive laundry and home services platform called "Laundry Care - Spin Cycle Solutions." The app is designed to provide users with a seamless and efficient way to manage their clothing care and household needs.

Key Features & Functionality:

  • Diverse Service Selection: Users can easily choose from a range of services, including Pick & Drop options like Dry Cleaning and Cloth Wash, as well as Home Maid services (e.g., Home cleaning, Cooking).
  • Intuitive Ordering Process: The app features a straightforward flow that allows users to select items based on clothing categories (MEN, WOMEN, KID, OTHER) and specific types (Jeans, Shirt, Uni-Form, Suit, T-Shirt, etc.) with clear pricing and quantity adjustments.
  • Efficient Cart Management: The system clearly displays the running total of added items and provides a dedicated option to View Cart and finalize the order.
  • Comprehensive Navigation: A robust side menu ensures quick access to all essential features, including the HomeScreen, Add-Task, Profile, Bucket (Cart), Backups, Rate this App, and Contact information.
  • User Experience: The design employs a clean, modern interface with relevant iconography and a focused user flow, enhancing the overall experience for scheduling and managing laundry and home tasks.
This project demonstrates proficiency in building user-centric mobile applications with multiple service offerings and robust order management capabilities.
