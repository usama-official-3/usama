ArabiaWheel Mobile App - Android & iOS

This project is the native mobile application for ArabiaWheel, a leading automotive platform in Bahrain, available on both Android and iOS. The app extends the full functionality of the web platform to mobile users, focusing on an optimized experience for buying, selling, and comparing vehicles on the go.

Key Features & Mobile-Specific Functionality:

  • Intuitive Home Screen: Allows users to quickly search for Used Cars or New Cars and browse by popular makes like Toyota, Hyundai, Honda, etc. It also provides immediate access to core functions: Free Ads, Verified Showrooms, and Auction bidding.
  • Advanced Car Listings: The main listings page offers a streamlined view with essential details (price in BHD, location, year, mileage, fuel type) and clear filtering options (Price, Location, Car Info) for quick search refinement.
  • Detailed Listing View: Each vehicle page displays high-resolution photo galleries, comprehensive car details, and direct communication options to Call seller or Send Message (WhatsApp integration).
  • Seamless Selling Process: The Sell Your Car module guides users through submitting necessary car information, including photos, city, color, and setting the Expected Bid and Minimum Bid for auction listings.
  • Car Comparison Tool: A dedicated feature allows users to easily compare two new car models side-by-side, along with a feed of Hot Car Comparisons (e.g., Nissan X-Trail vs. Toyota Rav4).
  • User Profile & Monetization: The 'My Plans' section, accessible via the user profile, showcases subscription tiers (Basic, Premium) with transparent feature lists (e.g., credits, listings, real-time lookup), facilitating in-app purchases.
  • Extended Mobile Navigation: The 'More' menu provides quick access to a personal Dashboard, Saved items, Blogs, Car Comparisons, and the ability to switch Language. The profile also allows users to manage My Ads and Saved Ads.
  • Showroom Directory: Features a searchable list of Verified Showrooms (e.g., Al Asfoor Car, Albahdar Cars) with ratings, location, and contact details.
This mobile application showcases expertise in native app development, complex e-commerce features, user authentication, and multi-faceted content delivery for the automotive industry.
