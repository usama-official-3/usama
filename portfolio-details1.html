ArabiaWheel

This project is a dynamic, full-featured web application, ArabiaWheel, which functions as an end-to-end automotive marketplace. It caters to buyers and sellers interested in Used Cars, New Cars, and Car Auctions, primarily serving the market in Bahrain. The platform is designed to provide users with a comprehensive, transparent, and seamless experience for all their car-related needs.

Key Features & Modules:

  • Used Car and New Car Listings: Features robust search and filtering tools (by city, make, model, price range) to help buyers find their "next match." Listings display detailed car information, pricing (BHD), and contact options to Show Phone Number or Send Message to the seller.
  • Car Auctions: A dedicated section showcases active car auctions, complete with expected bid prices, car details, and remaining time for bidding.
  • Sell My Car Functionality: Provides a simple, three-step process to list a car for sale: Enter Car Information, Upload Photos, and Enter Selling Price, making it easy for private sellers to connect with buyers.
  • Showrooms & Dealers: A directory of All Used Car Showrooms and dealers, complete with location and contact information, allowing users to search by name and city.
  • Monetization & Plans: A user-friendly 'My Plans' section presents clear tiered subscription options (Free, Basic, Premium) for sellers, detailing credits, listing limits, and enhanced features like Data enhancement and Real-time lookup.
  • Engaging Content: Includes a Blog for popular articles and industry news (e.g., 'Manama Auto Scene'), and a Videos section featuring reviews and featured car content to keep users engaged and informed.
  • User Profile: Features a personalized profile area for managing My Ads, My Saved Ads, and My Plans.
The application demonstrates strong expertise in e-commerce platform development, database management for large inventory, dynamic filtering, and implementing a subscription-based business model.
